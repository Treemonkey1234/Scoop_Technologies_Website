About Us

Scoop Technologies LLC is a forward-thinking technology company tackling the challenge of online identity verification.

Our mobile app, Scoop, unifies users' social media profiles into one verified platform, enhancing trust and authenticity in digital interactions. We empower individuals to showcase a comprehensive online presence while providing businesses with tools for real-time event tracking and engagement.

By offering robust verification and customizable privacy settings, Scoop ensures user safety and control over their digital identity. We aim to revolutionize online identity verification and event management, paving the way for more secure and authentic digital experiences.

Our Mission

To revolutionize online identity verification and create a more authentic digital world where trust is the foundation of all interactions.
